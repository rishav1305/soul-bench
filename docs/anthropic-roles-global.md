### Solutions Architect, Applied AI -- Bangalore (DETAILED)

This is the **highest-fit role currently available in India**.

**Requirements:**
- 10+ years in technical customer-facing roles (Solutions Architect, Sales Engineer, Technical Account Manager)
- Enterprise customer experience in India or APAC region
- Cloud architecture design and enterprise system integration experience
- Python proficiency
- Familiarity with LLM frameworks, machine learning, or data science
- Teaching and mentoring orientation
- Regional travel within India and APAC
- 25% in-office minimum (hybrid)

**Fit Analysis:**
- MATCHES: Python proficiency, LLM/agent development experience, cloud architecture (soul-cloud), enterprise system integration, teaching orientation
- GAP: The role asks for 10+ years in customer-facing roles specifically. Your background is engineering-first with consulting overlay (Gartner/Bitwise). Still strong but need to frame consulting experience prominently.
- This maps well to your soul-os ecosystem: architecture decisions, evaluation frameworks, integration patterns -- all things you have built.

## Section 5: Application Strategy for India-Based Candidates

### Tier 1: Apply Immediately (India-Based)

**Solutions Architect, Applied AI -- Bangalore**
- This is the only current role that does not require relocation
- Requires 10+ years in customer-facing technical roles
- Frame your experience: Technology Leader at Gartner (enterprise advisory), soul-os (full production AI system), soul-outreach (SaaS product), soul-cloud (cloud architecture), soul-agents (agent frameworks)
- Key narrative: "I have been living in Claude's ecosystem -- building a 31-project AI-native OS powered by Claude, running 14 Claude Code subagents, evaluating models with custom CARS metrics. I understand Claude's capabilities at a depth few candidates will match."

### Tier 2: Apply for Relocation Roles (Visa Sponsored)

**Forward Deployed Engineer, Applied AI -- London (PRIMARY TARGET)**
- Strongest technical match outside US
- 225,000-255,000 GBP salary (~$285k-$325k USD)
- Anthropic provides visa sponsorship + immigration lawyer
- Requirements: 4+ years customer-facing technical + production LLM experience
- Your edge: You have BOTH engineering depth AND consulting/advisory experience
- soul-agents (agent development), soul-outreach (production LLM app), soul-bench/soul-eval (evaluation frameworks)

**Forward Deployed Engineer, Applied AI -- Tokyo**
- Same role, APAC timezone (closer to India)
- Visa sponsorship available
- Smaller team = more visibility

**Product Engineer, Applied AI -- Seoul**
- Technical advisor role, similar to FDE but product-focused
- Seoul is newest office = ground-floor opportunity
- Korea has 6x growth in Claude Code users in 4 months

### Tier 3: Expression of Interest / Future Pipeline

**Engineering roles that may open in India (3-6 month horizon):**
- Applied AI Engineer -- likely to come to Bangalore as Applied AI team scales in APAC
- Product Engineer, Applied AI -- already in Seoul, may expand to India
- Developer Community Lead -- already in Tokyo, India is larger developer market
- Claude Evangelist -- strong fit with your content/outreach work

**Why engineering roles will likely come to India:**
1. India is 2nd largest Claude market globally
2. Claude Code adoption growing 10x in India
3. India's IT services industry (TCS, Infosys, Wipro, etc.) are key partners
4. The office announcement said "wide array of roles" -- not just GTM
5. Bengaluru is a known engineering talent hub, not a sales-only city
6. Similar APAC offices (Seoul, Tokyo) already have Product Engineer and FDE roles

### Tier 4: US Roles (Long Shot but Highest Ceiling)

- Anthropic sponsors H-1B visas (44 H-1B applications filed historically per MyVisaJobs)
- Most competitive roles: Agent Infrastructure, Claude Code, Agent SDK
- These require US relocation; visa process adds 3-6 months
- Worth applying in parallel -- if selected, they handle the immigration

### Application Tactics

1. **Apply to Bangalore SA role FIRST** -- this is the path of least resistance. No visa needed. Direct India hire.

2. **Apply to London FDE simultaneously** -- this is the strongest technical match in a location that sponsors visas.

3. **Apply to 2-3 US roles in parallel** -- Agent Infrastructure, Claude Code, FDE US. Cast a wide net.

4. **Tailor resume for each application type:**
   - SA roles: Lead with consulting/advisory experience, enterprise architecture, customer outcomes
   - FDE roles: Lead with production code, agent development, MCP/tool-use, Python/TypeScript
   - Engineering roles: Lead with distributed systems, container orchestration, evaluation frameworks

5. **Portfolio assets to highlight:**
   - soul-os: 6,700 Python + 3,000 React lines, production AI-native OS
   - soul-agents: YAML agents with boundary enforcement (maps to AI safety)
   - soul-mesh: Hub election, WebSocket sync, NAT relay (distributed systems)
   - soul-bench/soul-eval: CARS metric, benchmark design (evaluation expertise)
   - soul-cloud: Container-per-user PaaS (sandboxing, isolation)
   - 14 Claude Code subagents: Power user signal
   - 31 project ecosystem: Architectural thinking at scale

6. **Networking targets:**
   - Irina Ghose (MD India) -- LinkedIn connection
   - India-based Anthropic employees (via LinkedIn)
   - Applied AI team members (Solutions Architects, FDEs)
   - Referral from any current employee carries significant weight

7. **Timeline:**
   - Week 1: Polish resume, prepare portfolio links (GitHub repos)
   - Week 2: Apply to Bangalore SA + London FDE + 2 US roles
   - Week 3: Connect with Anthropic India team on LinkedIn
   - Week 4: Follow up, prepare for technical interviews
