## The Paradigm Shift

Rishav's professional identity has fundamentally changed. He is no longer a traditional coder who happens to use AI. He is an **AI-augmented architect** — someone who thinks in systems, directs AI coding tools to build them, and draws on 6 years of data engineering domain knowledge to evaluate and guide the output.

**What changed:**
- 90% of implementation is done through AI coding tools (Claude Code heavily, Google Copilot, Cline, Kilo Code)
- The real skill is: problem decomposition, system architecture, AI tool configuration, and domain expertise
- He can build full-stack applications, distributed systems, desktop apps — but not by hand-coding them
- He DOES write Python, SQL, and shell scripts directly (6 years of hands-on proficiency)
- 31 interconnected projects in the Soul ecosystem demonstrate the output

**What stays the same:**
- Engineer/Consultant/Researcher as the three career pillars (these are the VISION, not legacy labels)
- 6 years of real data engineering experience (Novartis, Gartner, IBM-TWC, Polestar)
- Domain expertise in data platforms, healthcare analytics, enterprise AI
