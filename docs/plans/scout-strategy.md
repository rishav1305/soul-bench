## 3. Application Narrative

### Resume Headline
> AI Platform Engineer | Built enterprise agentic AI serving 5,000+ users | Open-source LLM evaluation framework creator

### Cover Letter Hook (Adaptable Template)

**For AI/Platform roles:**
> At Gartner, I built an agentic AI platform that 5,000+ associates now use daily — 88% of their queries resolved without human intervention. That taught me what most AI projects miss: production AI isn't about models, it's about systems that stay reliable at scale. I bring 8 years of data engineering and a track record of shipping AI systems that enterprises actually depend on.

**For Data Engineering + AI hybrid roles:**
> I've spent 8 years building data platforms — from Novartis (15 pharmaceutical brands, 99.5% accuracy) to Gartner (serverless re-architecture saving 35% on infrastructure). Now I apply that same production discipline to AI systems: multi-agent orchestration, LLM evaluation frameworks, and distributed compute meshes. I don't just build pipelines — I build the infrastructure AI runs on.

**For Freelance/Consulting pitches:**
> I recently helped IBM-TWC cut data processing time by 60% and infrastructure costs by 30%. Before that, I launched Gartner's internal AI assistant to 5,000+ users. I specialize in taking AI from prototype to production — the hard part most teams struggle with. Happy to discuss how I can help with [specific project/problem].

**For Research-adjacent roles:**
> I created CARS (Cost-Aware Reasoning Score) — an efficiency metric for evaluating local LLM inference that balances quality against compute cost. Backed by a 10-task benchmark suite with Claude baselines. My research focus is practical: how do you measure whether an AI system is worth running?

### Cover Note Formula (3 lines)
```
Line 1: Specific proof point relevant to THEIR role/company
Line 2: What I bring that's different (systems thinking + data engineering + AI)
Line 3: What I want to discuss / why I'm reaching out
```

## 4. Daily SCOUT Routine

| Action | Time | Details |
|--------|------|---------|
| Review inbound matches (Instahyre, Wellfound, Naukri) | 45 min | Respond to recruiter messages within 24h. Flag quality matches. |
| Apply to 3-5 quality full-time roles | 90 min | Customize cover note for each using the hook template above. |
| Apply to 1-2 freelance opportunities + 3 LinkedIn DMs | 60 min | Direct outreach to hiring managers, CTOs, AI leads. |
| **Weekly:** Review tracker, follow-ups, update spreadsheet | 30 min | Friday evening. Track conversion rates. |

**Total:** ~3.25h on application days, ~30min on review-only days.
