### Campaign 8: Pharma / Healthcare AI

- **Targets:** Novartis (existing connections), Roche, Pfizer, AstraZeneca, Sanofi, Tempus, Recursion, Insilico Medicine, PathAI
- **Contact target:** 30-40

- **India angle:** Major pharma companies have India R&D hubs — high priority

### Campaign 9: Indian AI-First Companies

- **Targets:** Fractal Analytics, Mu Sigma, Tiger Analytics, LatentView, Sigmoid, Hasura, Postman, Razorpay (AI team), Zerodha (infra team), PhonePe, CRED, Meesho

### Campaign 10: Edge / IoT / Distributed Systems

- **Targets:** Cloudflare (Workers AI), Fastly, Fly.io, Balena, Particle, Arduino (Pro), Edge Impulse, Qualcomm AI
